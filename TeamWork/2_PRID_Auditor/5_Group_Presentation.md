# 5. Group Presentation of the Project

## 5.1 Presentation Guidelines
- Effectively communicate objectives, methodology, key findings, and conclusions.
- Use visual aids (slides, demonstrations).
- Allocate time for Q&A and feedback.
- **Participation:** 2nd Year students and faculty members (suggested compulsory).
- **Quality:** Demonstrate confidence, professionalism, and thorough grasp of the subject.

## 5.2 Audience
- Department/Institute students.
- Compulsory for 2nd Year students of the same branch.
- Faculty members.
