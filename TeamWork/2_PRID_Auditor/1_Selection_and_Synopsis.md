# 1. Selection of Project Problem and Submission of Synopsis

## 1.1 Submission Deadline
- **End of 2nd Week of the Session**

## 1.2 Guidelines
- Selection should align with interests, academic goals, and industry trends.
- Consideration of feasibility, scope, timeframe, and resources.
- Aim to address real-world problems or demonstrate innovative solutions.
- **Group size:** 02 to 04 members (Exceptional cases require recorded reasons).

## 1.3 Submission Pattern (02 to 04 Pages)
The synopsis must include:
1. Project Title
2. Group Members and Guide Name
3. Objectives of the project
4. Project Scope/Impact/Relevance
5. Project Deliverables
6. Execution Plan and its timeline
7. Technologies to be used
8. Financial Requirements (if any)
9. Other sections as required
