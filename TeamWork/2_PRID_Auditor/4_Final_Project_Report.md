# 4. Preliminary Draft & Final Copy of the Final Project Report

## 4.1 Submission Deadline
- **Preliminary Copy:** End of 12th to 13th Week of the Session.

## 4.2 Formatting Guidelines
- **Paper Size:** A4
- **Margins:** Left: 25mm | Right: 17.5mm | Top/Bottom: 17.5mm
- **Font Style:** Times New Roman
- **Font Sizes:**
  - Section Heading: 17pt, bold, leading caps
  - 2nd Level: 14pt, bold, sentence case
  - 3rd Level: 12pt, bold, sentence case
  - Running Text: 12pt normal
  - Captions: 11pt
- **Line Spacing:** 1.5

## 4.3 Report Structure
The Final Report should contain:
1. Cover Page (Title, Students, Branch, Institute, Logo, Year)
2. Title Page
3. Certificate (Guide & HOD signed)
4. Declaration
5. Acknowledgements
6. Abstract
7. Table of Contents
8. List of Figures & Tables
9. Nomenclature/Abbreviations
10. **Report Text (Chapters)**
11. References

## 4.4 Submission Note
- Draft is for feedback.
- Final copy must be Hard-Bound.
- Chapters onward should be printed on both sides.
