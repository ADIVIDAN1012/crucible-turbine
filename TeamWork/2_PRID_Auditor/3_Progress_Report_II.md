# 3. Submission of Progress Report-II and Work Done Review

## 3.1 Submission Deadline
- **End of 10th Week of the Session**

## 3.2 Guidelines
- Demonstrate significant advancements since Report-I.
- Include updated timelines and indication of achieved milestones.
- Provide insights into refinements based on feedback.

## 3.3 Submission Pattern (07-10 Pages)
1. Project Title
2. Group Members and Guide Name
3. **Objectives Achieved:** Recap and evaluation of phase goals.
4. **Significant Milestones:** Highlights and implications.
5. **Refinements and Enhancements:** Lessons learned and feedback integration.
6. **Remaining Tasks:** Identification of final requirements.
7. **Timeline Update:** Finalized deadlines for completion.
8. **Further Work:** Final steps before report submission.
